## Scene Structure (30-Min Teaching Video)

> Use the course script as source; only extract what you need.

### Scene 1 – Hook (0:00–1:30)
- Visual: Dark to light gradient, soft movement
- Text: "Are you carrying {{ROOT_CAUSE}}?"
- VO: 2–4 lines naming the pain + promise of the session

### Scene 2 – Title Card (1:30–2:00)
- Text:
  - Rise to Thrive Academy
  - Mini-Course {{NUMBER}}
  - {{COURSE_TITLE}}

### Scene 3 – Empathy & Story (2:00–6:00)
- Use the "story" or "pain framing" from the script
- 1–2 brief vignettes, not the entire script

### Scene 4 – What This Really Is (6:00–10:00)
- Explain the root cause:
  - 2–3 core traits
  - What it feels like mentally, emotionally, physically
  - 1 line about spiritual warfare connection

### Scene 5 – Spiritual Warfare Lens (10:00–15:00)
- Expose 1–2 lies of the enemy
- Overlay 1–2 scriptures used in the script
- Keep visuals calm and reverent

### Scene 6 – Framework / Steps (15:00–22:00)
Create 3–5 steps from the script content:
1. Awareness
2. Alignment with truth
3. Action / boundary
4. Reinforcement / practice
5. Community / support

### Scene 7 – Guided Practice (22:00–26:00)
- 1 reflection prompt
- 1 simple embodied pause (breath, posture, etc.)
- 1 sentence inviting Holy Spirit into the moment

### Scene 8 – Prayer + CTA (26:00–30:00)
- Use closing prayer from script (summarized)
- CTA:
  - "Continue your healing in the 5-Day Foundation Course"
  - Show URL or QR code
